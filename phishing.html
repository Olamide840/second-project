Phishing Awareness

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a form of social engineering and a scam where attackers deceive people into revealing sensitive information or installing malware such as viruses, worms, adware, or ransomware.

How to Spot Phishing:

  • Look for suspicious email addresses or domains.
  • Watch out for urgency — “Your account will be closed!”
  • Do not click unknown links or attachments.
  • Check for bad grammar or weird requests.
  • Urgent or emotionally appealing language, especially messages that claim dire consequences for not responding immediately

    Prevention:

  • Avoid clicking suspicious links or opening attachments in emails
  • Always verify the legitimacy of a request before providing personal information
  • Never click any links or attachments in suspicious emails or Teams messages
