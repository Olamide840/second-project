Phishing Awareness

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where hackers trick you into giving away personal info like passwords or credit card numbers by pretending to be a trustworthy source.

How to Spot Phishing:

  • Look for suspicious email addresses or domains.
  • Watch out for urgency — “Your account will be closed!”
  • Don’t click unknown links or attachments.
  • Check for bad grammar or weird requests.
